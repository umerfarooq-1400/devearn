5 Websites You Can Build in One Day and Sell

July 2025 · 6 min read · Business Ideas

You don't need weeks to build a sellable website. Most small businesses in Pakistan need simple, clean sites that communicate what they do and how to contact them. That's it. Here are 5 types you can build in a single day — and sell for Rs 8,000 to Rs 25,000 each.

How This Works

Build one great template for each category. Then resell it to different businesses in that niche — just swap logo, colors, and content each time. One day of work becomes a repeatable income stream.

The 5 Website Types

01

Restaurant / Cafe Website

Rs 8,000 – 15,000

Every restaurant, dhaba, and cafe in Pakistan needs an online presence. Most have a Facebook page but no real website. A simple site with their menu, location, and contact number is enough to charge good money.

Build Time
3–5 hours
Pages Needed
Home, Menu, Contact
Where to Find Clients
Local cafes, food delivery pages
Upsell Opportunity
Online ordering (+Rs 10,000)
02

Clothing Brand / Boutique

Rs 12,000 – 25,000

Karachi and Lahore are full of small clothing brands selling on Instagram. They desperately need a proper website to look legitimate and stop losing sales to competitors who have one. This is a goldmine niche.

Build Time
4–6 hours
Pages Needed
Home, Shop, About, Contact
Where to Find Clients
Instagram clothing pages, Facebook shops
Upsell Opportunity
WhatsApp checkout integration
03

Doctor / Clinic Website

Rs 15,000 – 30,000

Doctors and clinics are high-value clients who pay well and rarely negotiate on price. They need a professional looking site that shows services, timings, and an appointment contact. They also refer you to other doctors — one client becomes five.

Build Time
4–5 hours
Pages Needed
Home, Services, Timings, Contact
Where to Find Clients
Local clinics, hospital directories
Upsell Opportunity
Appointment booking form
04

Wedding / Events Photographer

Rs 10,000 – 20,000

Photographers need a portfolio website to show their work and attract bookings. Most are using Instagram which doesn't look professional for high paying clients. A clean portfolio site with a contact form is exactly what they need.

Build Time
3–4 hours
Pages Needed
Home, Portfolio, Packages, Contact
Where to Find Clients
Instagram photographers, Facebook events groups
Upsell Opportunity
Gallery with password protection
05

Real Estate Agent / Property

Rs 20,000 – 40,000

Real estate agents in Pakistan pay the most for websites because their clients are high value. A property listing site with WhatsApp contact integration is extremely easy to build and commands premium pricing because the agent sees it as an investment not an expense.

Build Time
5–7 hours
Pages Needed
Home, Listings, About, Contact
Where to Find Clients
Zameen.com agents, local property offices
Upsell Opportunity
Monthly maintenance Rs 2,000/month

The Strategy

Don't try to sell all 5 at once. Pick one niche, build one perfect template for it, then approach 10 businesses in that niche with the same offer. Once you close 2-3 clients in one niche you have reviews, a refined template, and a clear pitch.

Then move to the next niche and repeat.

Quick Math

If you close just 2 restaurant websites per month at Rs 12,000 each — that's Rs 24,000 extra income. Part time, from home, using free tools. That's a realistic starting point for any Pakistani web developer.
